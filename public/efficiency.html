Under the hood

Engineered for lean performance

  • Smart caching: frequently accessed files & thumbnails are cached near users to cut latency.
  • In‑memory compression: compress on the fly to reduce bandwidth and memory pressure.
  • Just‑in‑time loading: stream and load only what is required at any moment.
  • Backpressure aware I/O: avoids overfilling buffers when many users are active.
Why it matters

No clogging as you scale

Whether it's 10 files or 10,000, Lightshare is designed to stay responsive. Efficient resource usage means smooth performance on modest hardware and lower cloud bills.
